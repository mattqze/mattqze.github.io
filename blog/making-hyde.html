What is Hyde?

Hyde is a blogging platform based on Jekyll.

Why make Hyde?

I was sick of messing with awful configuration files. In fact, the configuration is so shotty, I broke my Jekyll site in the first 5 minutes of configuration.
(Seriously, it's stuck on the default 'Github User' author name.) So I decided to make Jekyll in PHP.
Based on Minima, the default theme. Themeing needs work but eventually might be possible.
The only problem with Jekyll themes and their conversions to CSS is the fact that Jekyll themes, can re-arrange a site's layout. CSS, on it's own, cannot do this.
It can also change naming conventions for elements' classes. No bueno for my rip-off. I'd like to make a naming convention but that goes directly against what Hyde stands for.
For now though, all themes are based upon Minima. There's a pretty sick Catppuccin Mocha theme included too.
